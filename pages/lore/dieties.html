Greater Dieties


It should be stated that the alignment system is there for those who use it. As a DM, I will be ignoring it, as I find it more constricting than informative.

**Uesis, the Creator and God King

Domains: Life, Order
Alignment: Lawful Good
Symbol: ***
Uesis is the original god, the true creator of Khaldûn. He shaped the world, welcomed the other gods, and made the **First Races. He is actively involved in the affairs of Khaldûn, to the disdain of some people. The Kohan** worship Uesis as their primary god, and shun the Fallen**.

**Fandir, the Conservationist

Domains: Nature, Tempest
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Symbol: ***
Fandir was the second god, following in the footsteps of Uesis. He guides the woodlands and the creatures that live within, including the humanoids created to live there. In the conflict betwee the Kohan** and Fallen**, Fandir generally does not take a side and tends to leave nature to itself, only taking revenge when his lands are tampered with.

**Akrua, the Warriess

Domain: War
Alignment: True Neutral
Symbol: ***
Akrua is the Goddess of War. She thrives off of battle, and as such, is undefeated. She takes pleasure in the violence mortal - it sustains her vitality. In fact, she has such a large influence on the peoples of Khaldûn, she does not need to intervene personally in the world; she made her impression eons ago. Warriors on every side worship her, hoping for her blessing in their coming battles. In terms of the **Eternal Confict, she does not take a side, instead relishing the bloodshed occurring on each side.


**Irollan, the Sun God

Domains: Light, Order
Alignment: Lawful Good
Symbol: ***
Irollan, being quite literally the God of the Sun, is also the son of Uesis. He ensures that the sun continues it usual journey unopposed across the sky. (Worshippers)*** As the only true blood relative of the God King, he sides with his father opposing the **Fallen.

**Tudalle, the Craftsman

Domains: Arcana, Forge
Alignment: Lawful Neutral
Symbol: ***
Tudalle is the God of the Forge, fashioning and imbuing weapons, tools and other contraptions for all of the gods. His wisdom and technical skill is called upon by artisans and craftsmen alike, as well as artificiers. In the **Eternal Conflict, like his borther, Pyrus, Tudalle refuses to participate in any kind; his creations are on both sides, and does not wish to see any of them destroyed. However, he does ask that his worshippers participate in the war, so that they can show their technical skill and prowess, honoring him.

**Pyrus, the Fire Mage

Domains: Arcana, Knowledge
Alignment: Chaotic Good
Symbol: ***
Pyrus is the God of Magic and Knowledge, known especially for his usage of fire magic. Mages and scholars turn to Pyrus for his intelligence and wisdom. Following the lead of his twin brother, Pyrus himself does not participate in the **Eternal Conflict. Instead, he calls his followers into the fight in order to honor him.


**Biara, the Thief

Domain: Trickery
Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
Symbol: ***
Biara is the God of Thievery and Cunning, worshipped by all who need stealth and cunning in their line of work. She herself is a kleptomaniac, always stealing things from the other gods. As such, no god wants her on their side. Instead, she asks her followers to use this conflict as an opportunity to further their goals.

**Ahriman, the Fallen One

Domain: Life
Alignment: Chaotic Evil
Symbol: ***
Ahriman, widely called "The Fallen God", is the centerpiece of the **Cult of the Fallen. His followers, **the Fallen, have broken away from the Kohan, and want nothing to do with Uesis or Irollan. Ahriman created life unlike anything produced by Uesis; Abberations, Fiends and Monstrosities. After his designs were rejected by Uesis, Ahriman defied his orders, creating them anyway. As such, Ahriman was formally stripped of his title as Caregiver of Khaldûn, a grave insult. Ahriman declared Uesis to be a god blinded by his ego and status, unworthy to be followed or worshipped, and declared a war upon Uesis that will can only end in two ways: his death, or Uesis' death.

**Ornella, the Goddess of Death

Domains: Death, Grave
Alignment: True Neutral
Symbol: ***
Ornella is the Goddess of Death and the Dead. She guides all who die to the afterlife, as well as maintains control in her **Final Kingdom. All worship her; courting her ire may destroy one's chance at entrance to her kingdom. She also is among the majority of the Greater Dieties** who stay neutral in the **Eternal Conflict. The war produces death, adding souls to her kingdom. There is no need for her to intervene.
