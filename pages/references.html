Battle of Northista: One of the most important battles to the Ceyah. In it, the Ceyah lost one of their best warriors, and were nearly wiped out. Their victory signaled that the Ceyah were no longer a small movement - it was large enough to truly challenge both the Kohan and the Fallen.

Pyrus Sect: The religion organization devoted to the worship of Pyrus.

Celestials: The servants of the gods, created jointly by Uesis and Ornella. They were originally made for Ornella, to help guide souls to the Final Kingdom. Since then, their usage has expanded to serve all gods, even Ahriman. Generally, no matter what god they serve, they all appear relatively the same: generally in a humanoid shape, wearing a brown cloak, with the emblem of their god emblazened on the front of their cloak. They float above the ground, despite their shadow showing them with legs. Their face is completley obscured; when one looks into their hood, all one sees is blackness. They have a set of flaming wings sprouting from their back, with the fire burning brighter along the bone lines.

Ceyah Rebellion: The Ceyah Rebellion is break between the Ceyah and the Kohan/Fallen. It has two distinct endings: the first when Ahriman dictated that the Fallen make peace with the Ceyah, and the second when Uesis dictated the same thing to the Kohan.

Divine Palace: The Divine Palace is not only the resident of seven of the gods (not Biara nor Ahriman), but it is also the name of the plane on which those seven gods reside.

Final Kingdom: The Final Kingdom is the plane of the dead. Departed souls from Khaldûn are guided by Ornella's Celestials to the plane, where their afterlife awaits.

First Races: The First Races are the original humanoids made by Uesis, namely, dwarves, elves, humans and orcs.

Khaldûn Pantheon: The pantheon of gods led by Uesis.

Life Day: Life Day is the day marking the anniversary of the creation of the First Races.

Original Eight: The Original Eight are the first 8 gods: Uesis, Fandir, Akrua, Tudalle, Pyrus, Biara, Ahriman and Ornella. In other words, it is all of the gods except Irollan.

Silent Stalkers: The Silent Stalkers was the Ceyah division under the command of Divsha Poundfoot, known for destroying Kohan and Fallen supply lines.
