Session 0
  • Character Creation
    • Free feat - no variant humans
    • No homebrew, check with me for Unearthed Arcana
    • Multiclass only for role-playing or background reasons
    • Level 2
    • Roll for health - everyone rerolls 1's, d10's and d12's also reroll 2's
    • I would suggest making them well-rounded. There will be combat, but also a fair amount of other checks...
    • Casters - keep track of casting time
    • Email me written backstories, character descriptions, saving throws, passive perception and insight.
  • Divine Inspiration
    • At the end of every session, there will be a vote for the best creativity or role-playing in the session. The winner will get a point of Divine Inspiration which they will have to use before the end of the next session (or it is lost)
    • I (the DM) may award multiple at the end of a session if I feel that there were multiple events that deserve it.
    • This may be used on ANY D20 role, whether I (the DM) or on one of the players after the outcome of the initial role has been decided.
    • If one has advantage or disadvantage, both dice will be rerolled.
    • The player will decide which outcome happens
    • In the event of a tie when voting, all of the people that have tied will receive divine inspiration. However, if I (the DM) feel that this rule is abused, I (the DM) will break the tie.
  • World and Lore
    • Backstories - everyone will be part of the Adventurer's Guild (have your own choice as to why)
    • Any lore based questions?
  • Table Rules
    • Some +1 weapons may not be magical - rather, they will be "mastercrafted". As such, these weapons can be identified by anyone with at least martial proficiency in them.
    • Generally, ignore material components for spells unless the material has a gold value. If there is a gold value to the material cost of a spell, you will need to either:
      1. have that specific material in your inventory
      2. OR have the gold necessary to buy said material. Upon casting the spell, that price of the material will be deducted from your gold amount. In game, it will be described as you had already bought that item last time you were intown.
    • Battlemap link
    • Using Revised Martial Equipment in terms of combat gear stats and abilities
    • Will be using the rules outlines in "Best Practice" - for ease of play, will be putting them here (with a couple of wording tweaks)

      • Flanking an enemy adds +2 to attack rolls for two creatures flanking an enemy, and +5 if three or more creatures flank an enemy.

      • Dual Wielding and Double Ended attacks count the off hand attack as part of your attack action, rather than using a Bonus Action for characters that have the feat Dual Wielder.

      • If you have the Grappler or Tavern Brawler feat and Martial training with unarmed attacks, you can make unarmed deflect, trip, and disarm attempts.

      • In this system, Martial training Unarmed Strikes deal d4 bludgeoning damage. To balance any race which grants natural weapons such as Tabaxi claws, Lizardfolk bite, or Dragonborn claws, they instead deal one size larger dice. A Tabaxi's claws would deal d6 slashing damage, for example.

      • When you score a critical hit, maximize your weapon's dice damage, then roll the bonus damage from a critical hit to prevent Critical Hits dealing less damage than normal. Do not maximize attachers such as Sneak Attack or Smite.

      • During a grapple, Heavy or Two Handed weapons attack with disadvantage, while Light, Unarmed Strikes, and Bite attacks gain Advantage. This also has an added benefit of making weapons with Versatile more versatile!

Session 1

Important Document: Astrakane Quest Scroll (In Fuyuki's possession)

  • Started off on the road to Astrakane, a village in the western edge of Lakeside Forest near Spathi
    • Fuyuki unnervingly felt like he was being watched for most of the session (with various intensities)
    • When the party arrived at the village, people were hiding in their homes and dogs were missing.
  • Talked to the Mayor and Sheriff, found out that 3 people and all of the village dogs had gone missing in the last week.
    • Atresk, the Sheriff, joined the party for the duration of the investigation.
    • Talked to the Priest and the villagers. The party concluded that a beast was behind the disappearances
    • Fuyuki got a book regarding Uesis from the Priest
  • Went into the forest at dusk. After wandering for an hour, they were attacked by the largest wolf (about 8 feet tall on hind legs) any of them (including Atresk) had ever seen.
    • Varjo took some damage, and Atresk was nearly killed, but they brought down the massive wolf and three of the normal wolves. Atresk smashed the massive wolf's skull after taking a severe bit to the abdomen. The rest of the wolves fled upon the death of the massive wolf.
    • Fuyuki started tanning half of the hide and turned half of the meat into jerky.
  • After taking a long rest, the party searched the surrounding area, and found the wolf den, where they found three humanoid bodies.
    • After searching the den, they found conclusive evidence linking two of the bodies to missing people, and assumed the third body was the third missing person.
    • Went back to take the wolf head back to the village, but the massive wolf body had completely disappeared. The others normal sized wolf bodies were still there.
    • When returning to the village, the party saw a humanoid completely cloaked in black watching them. It ignored all attempts at communication, and left the scene before the party could reach it. Fuyuki's sense of being watched completely disappeared after this.
  • Returned to the village hall and informed the Mayor of what had occurred.
    • The party was promptly paid and the Mayor informed them that the Dohkes had returned (they essentially are Adventurer's Guild celebrities) and that Fashum Dohke wanted to meet them.
    • Party leveled up (to level 3)
    • Fuyuki (Luke) earned Divine Inspiration for his creative (and unexpected) use of rope
